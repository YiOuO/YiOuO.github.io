  1. DualSPHysics+: An Enhanced DualSPHysics with Improvements in Accuracy, Energy Conservation and Resolution of the Continuity Equation
    Yi Zhan, Min Luo, and Abbas Khayyer
    Computer Physics Communications, 2025
    Abs HTML PDF Code

    This paper presents an enhanced version of the well-known SPH open-source code DualSPHysics for the simulation of free-surface fluid flows. The enhancements include Optimized Particle Shifting, Riemann stabilization, and improved volume conservation schemes.
